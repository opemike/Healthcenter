OUR CANDIDATE

Explore the Services Offered by Rightchoice Healthcare!

At Rightchoice, we welcome both new and seasoned caregivers to join us in providing homecare to individuals in the comfort of their own homes. We seek caregivers who are open to training and committed to upholding our rigorous standards of care. For us, caregiving is more than just a job; it's a vocation. It involves not only delivering personal care but also creating a secure environment where our clients can lead their lives safely and feel genuinely valued.

Support Worker

Within the framework of Rightchoice Healthcare Policies and Procedures, our Support Workers collaborate as part of a team to deliver personalized support to adults and children with learning disabilities across various community and accommodation-based settings. Working in conjunction with those we support, the team, and other stakeholders, they contribute to the planning, organization, implementation, monitoring, and review of activities, striving to achieve the goals outlined in Person-Centered Plans, Individual Plans, and Health Action Plans .

Care Assistant

As a care assistant, you have the opportunity to positively impact the lives of vulnerable individuals in your local community, offering the support needed for them to live independently in their homes. With the appropriate qualifications, we provide comprehensive training to nurture exceptional caregivers. In recognition of their hard work, our carers receive competitive pay rates, an award-winning training program, additional training as needed, and a supportive environment. Many have chosen us as their preferred Care staffing agency.

Live-in Carer

Within the framework of Rightchoice Policies and Procedures, our live-in carers collaborate as part of a team to deliver individualized center care, including tasks like washing, dressing, supporting morning and nighttime routines, supervising medication, assisting with chores, and providing companionship.

Senior Healthcare Assistant

In alignment with our Policies and Procedures, senior healthcare assistants in our team lead other teams to ensure the care and support needs of our residents are met day and night. This involves providing personal care, addressing each resident's physical, emotional, and spiritual needs, and promoting their independence, choice, and control.

Home Managers

According to our Policies and Procedures, house managers in our supported living facilities take the lead in ensuring the well-being of residents. This includes addressing their physical, emotional, and social needs, overseeing the safe, smooth, and efficient operation of the care home, conducting assessments, and developing, implementing, and evaluating individualized care plans for each resident.
